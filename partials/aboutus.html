The Luv My Avo Story

“Luv My Avo was formed out of a passion for cooking nutritious, healthy food for our family. We love the taste and benefits of cooking with avocado oil but found that our local markets lacked quality products, let alone options that offered the variety of taste options and infused flavours. Admittedly it was a bit selfish, but we wanted access to affordable avocado oil-based products here at home and figured we were not alone. So why not make them ourselves?”

We have spent the better part of the last twenty years working in the hospitality, wellness and technology industries in both operational, marketing and sales roles. LuvMyAvo is committed to building a business that prioritizes our community impact as much as profitability and innovation. We believe our brand’s success is based on transparent relationships with our customers and vendors and by being responsive to their needs and preferences.

Luv My Avo is developing a range of products that we plan to launch within the next 6-12 months and will also look to acquire new brands and manufacturing technology to help grow our company and capitalize on new ingredients and trends.

Our Mission

Love My Avo strives to produce high-quality, affordable products using nourishing, healthy ingredients that are derived from sustainable farming practices. Our vision is to serve our community of passionate food-lovers with delicious products and extend that service to our friends and neighbours by providing support to local charities and food programs.

Make Food Not War

We believe that everyone deserves to have access to healthy meals. With that in mind, we have developed MakeFoodNotWar.org that will act as the philanthropic arm of LuvMyAvo with a goal of contributing resources to non-profits and as a strategic partner to charities in the Lower Mainland who run food programs, community outreach services and provide opportunities for those with employment barriers by way of culinary education.
